The Last Light

"The Last Light", a first-person sci-fi runner, made with C# in the Unity Engine. I spent about 4 weeks on the concept and the development itself. Initially, it was an assignment for one of my courses, Unity Game Scripting, but I decided I wanted to take it further and keep working on it after assessment.

Out of the Shadows

This was one of my projects from the year before I got admitted to CMGT at Saxion. It was my first "bigger" projects in the Unity Engine and in C#. It's a top-down stealth strategy game.

The Secrets of Ahpuch

This game was the 3rd project in my 1st year of my Game Development bachelor. Together with 5 fellow students, we combined our skills, with "The Secrets of Ah Puch" being the end result.

I was the only engineer/programmer in this project, together with 3 artists and 2 designers. It was made in the GxPEngine, which is a custom engine developed mainly by the teachers, and student feedback continually helps improve it.

The Legend of Leonard Danger

This project was the 2nd project I made with my fellow students. This time it was made in Processing, written with JavaScript.

Frogger 1981 remake

In the 2nd quartile of the 1st year, we were tasked to recreate an old arcade game, possibly with your own chosen theme.

At the time, I was quite intrigued by the Star Wars franchise, so I saw an opportunity and decided to remake Frogger, which originates from 1981, with a Star Wars theme. The art assets are all self-drawn, in PyxelEdit.
